Welcome to the Department of Computer Science In Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at Malla Reddy Engineering College. Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to the development of computer systems that mimic a human brain and enable them to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence. Machine learning is a specific form of AI that allows computers to learn and grow after they are introduced to scenarios in the form of data
